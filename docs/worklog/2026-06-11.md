## Decisions Made

- `Answer` deserves its own package; catlab examples depending on
  "numexpr-core" just for the IOU would have been conceptually wrong.
- One shared `catlab-portable` pair instead of per-example node/beam
  workspaces (6): the three examples form one Tier-3 family (one tier head on
  the site); the per-family surface keeps the demo honest at a fraction of
  the infrastructure.
- Model values live in the cores (`*.Models`), imported by both denotations —
  "same description" is literal, not a copy that can drift.
- New `Deferred` strings describe counts (Int), not floats — sidesteps
  purerl's verbose float `show` cosmetics.
