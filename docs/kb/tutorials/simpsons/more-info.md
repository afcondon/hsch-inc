## Makers
A project of the [Visualizing Urban Data ideaLab](http://vudlab.com/#/) at UC Berkeley. Created in [d3.js](http://d3js.org/) and [AngularJS](http://angularjs.org/) by [Lewis Lehe](https://twitter.com/LewisLehe) and [Victor Powell](http://vctr.me/). 

Lewis Lehe is a PhD student in Transportation Engineering at UC Berkeley, and Victor Powell is a freelance developer, JavaScript instructor and yinzer.
