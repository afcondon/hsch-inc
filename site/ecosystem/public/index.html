The PureScript Libraries Ecosystem

The Lonely Planet Guide to PureScript-landia

Not Pursuit (which, since it includes everything, emphasizes nothing). Not documentation. An opinionated, curated guide to the libraries that make PureScript worth learning — with type signatures you can read, compelling examples, and memorable framing.

Learning Haskell or PureScript is hard enough that people over-invest in the language and under-invest in the libraries. The inverse of Python, where the libraries are the reason to learn. This guide makes the case that PureScript's libraries are worth the climb.
